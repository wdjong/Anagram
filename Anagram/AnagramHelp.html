Anagram

Search for words in a combination of letters

  • Enter a combination of letters you'd like to have rearranged.
  • Choose Search from the Tools menu.
  • The computer finds words and adds them to the list.

Combine words to use up letters

  • Choose Combine from the Tools menu
  • The computer combines found words in combinations that use up all the letters.
  • Combinations appear in the list.

Timed 9-letter competition with computer

  • Choose Generate 9 from the Tools menu
  • The 9 letters are a rearranged 9 letter word.
  • Type words into the 'Your words' textbox. When you press [Enter] they are added to the list.
  • >Words should be four letters or more. (This can be adjusted)
  • The centre letter should be included.
  • Each letter may be used only once.
  • You can press backspace in the empty textbox to bring the last word back for editing.
  • You can remove a word from the list by highlighting it and pressing backspace
  • Normally, no colloquial or foreign words should be included however the program accepts anything that the Word Dictionary doesn't complain about.
  • Normally, no capitals, apostrophes or plural words ending in "s" should be included however the program accepts anything that the Word Dictionary doesn't complain about.
  • There is a ten minute limit by default (This is adjustable).
  • You should aim to get more than the computer in the time allowed.

Generate a 16-letter puzzle

  • Choose Generate 16 from the Tools menu
  • Type words into the 'Your words' textbox. When you press [Enter] they are added to the list.
  • The 16 letters are selected so that more frequently used letters are more likely to appear.

Commands

File menu

Exit

Closes the program

Tools

Search

Rearranges letters in the 'letters to rearrange' text box and finds words; adding them to a list.

Combine

Combines words from the 'Words found' list to use up all the letters in interesting ways.

Generate 9

Finds a 9 letter word and rearranges it. Traditionally the centre letter must be used in each word and word lenght is 4 or more.

Generate 16

Create a 16 letter grid. Traditionally words must be constructed from adjacent letters and word length is 3 or more.
